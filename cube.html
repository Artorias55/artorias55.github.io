Through an on-site installation, the audience can interact with the visual imagery centered around the black cube.

The visual imagery is generated and transformed in real-time based on the sounds of live improvisational performances captured by a microphone.

This project aims to explore the relationship between the performer and the audience, allowing viewers to enter the metaphorical inner space represented by the black cube. They can create visual changes based on their own feelings and dynamically participate in the performance.
