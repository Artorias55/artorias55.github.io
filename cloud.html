In the southeastern region of Guizhou Province, there is a concentration inhabited by the minority nationalities and Miaos account the most.

Deep in the moutains with poor access, this region is widely considered as cut off from the outside world. It is also enveloped in a shroud of mysrty legends, among which Miao Doctor plays a legendary role.

This project conducted on-site interviews with three Miao doctors of different age groups and varying levels of residential development. After spending a month living with and following them, we showcased the image of Miao doctors and the ethnic minority culture here through various media.
