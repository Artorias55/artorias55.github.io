This project is an experience-centered music interactive game where graphic illustration is combined with music production.

Many behaviors in life that can be associated with "sound" are accompanied by different styles of music, such as rock, jazz, ambient, electronic, etc.

Different interactive actions in the scene will trigger corresponding changes in the music.

The project consists of 10 separate game levels, divided into three categories: daily scenes, noisy scenes and mini-games.
